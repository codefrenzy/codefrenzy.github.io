Speaking

I’ve always loved sharing my knowledge and experiences with others, but I didn’t always have the courage to step onto a stage. In 2023, I decided to face my fear of public speaking head-on, embracing the mantra “practice makes better.” Since then, I’ve jumped at every opportunity to talk about topics I’m passionate about, and it’s been an incredibly rewarding journey. Engaging with diverse minds at conferences and meetups has been especially fulfilling.

Below is a list of the talks, panels, and podcasts I’ve participated in.

If you’d like me to speak at your event, please feel free to get in touch. I’d love to connect!
